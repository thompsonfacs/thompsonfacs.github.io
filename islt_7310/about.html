My name is Jamie Thompson and I am a Family and Consumer Sciences teacher. I teach at Battle High School in Columbia, MO. I have been teaching for ten years and look forward to many more. I love teaching practical skills that help students prepare for life beyond school. My favorite ways to teach are through hands-on learning and technology. In addition to teaching, I am pursuing a graduate degree in Learning Technologies and Design.
In the future, I hope to work as a Curriculum Director or Technology Specialist.I would love to use my passions to help other educators improve their instruction.

I earned my Bachelor of Science in Education from Missouri State University in Springfield, MO. I graduated in December 2010 with an emphasis in Family and Consumer Sciences. Currently, I am a graduate student at the University of Missouri in Columbia, MO. I am studying Learning Technologies and Design with an emphasis in Technology in Schools. I hope to complete my degree by May 2023.
