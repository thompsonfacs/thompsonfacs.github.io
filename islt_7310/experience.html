Family & Consumer Sciences Teacher

Holcomb High School, USD 363, Holcomb, KS

Fall 2011- Spring 2013

As the sole Family and Consumer Sciences teacher in the district, I taught a comprehensive program for 7th -12th grade students. I taught a wide range of courses, including Exploratory FCS, Consumer Education, Meal Management, Nutrition & Wellness, Human Growth & Development, and Housing Decisions & Design. The high school had a one-to-one technology program, so all my students had access to a school-provided laptop, which allowed me to begin my career with a strong technology emphasis. Edmodo was the primary Learning Management System used.

In addition to teaching I was also the FCCLA sponsor. During my time in Holcomb we were able to increase FCCLA membership from 6 to 25 students. In my second year, I also created a “Sunshine Committee” for the staff to boost morale and increase building engagement.

Family & Consumer Sciences Teacher

Fulton High School, Fulton Public Schools, Fulton, MO

Fall 2013- Spring 2017

As a Family and Consumer Sciences teacher at Fulton High School I taught a comprehensive program for 9th-12th grade students. I taught a wide range of courses, including Life Skills, Child Development, Family Living, Foods & Nutrition, Advanced Foods, and Housing & Interior Design. During my time in Fulton, the enrollment in the FACS program increased so dramatically that we were able to hire another full-time teacher and increase course offerings available to students. Advanced Foods was a class created under my leadership that allowed Seniors to take an additional culinary course and advance their skills in the kitchen.

Midway through my tenure, the high school became a one-to-one technology school and all students were provided with Chromebooks. This opportunity greatly increased my understanding and experience with the Google Education suite. Google Classroom was our primary Learning Management System. In addition to teaching, I also sponsored FCCLA, Hornets Helping Hornets, served as Prom Coordinator, and was a member of the Building Leadership Team.

Family & Consumer Sciences Teacher

Battle High School, Columbia Public Schools, Columbia, MO

Fall 2017- Present

At Battle High School I am one of three Family and Consumer Sciences teachers, so I have had the opportunity to specialize in teaching the courses that I enjoy most. I currently teach My Cuisine, Global Cuisine, and Interior Design I & II. Our high school is a one-to-one technology school, so all of my students have access to a school-provided laptop, which is frequently used in my classroom. During my time with Columbia Public Schools my ability to understand and incorporate classroom technology has improved dramatically. We use Schoology as our primary Learning Management System. I am skilled and confident in using the tools available through this site and have become a leader in my department to implement its use.

In addition to teaching I serve as a Practical Arts representative for the district’s Standards Referenced Grading initiative and am on the Culturally Responsive Teaching committee in our building. My love for curriculum and technology has blossomed during my time here and I have had the opportunity to write and develop several curriculum resources for the district’s Family and Consumer Sciences department. I have developed an asynchronous online summer school curriculum for FACS 101, several interactive digital notebooks to be used in the My Cuisine and Global Cuisine classroom, and various other online resources.
