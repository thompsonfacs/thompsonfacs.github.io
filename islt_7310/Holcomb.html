Family & Consumer Sciences Teacher

Holcomb High School, USD 363, Holcomb, KS

Fall 2011- Spring 2013

As the sole Family and Consumer Sciences teacher in the district, I taught a comprehensive program for 7th -12th grade students. I taught a wide range of courses, including Exploratory FCS, Consumer Education, Meal Management, Nutrition & Wellness, Human Growth & Development, and Housing Decisions & Design. The high school had a one-to-one technology program, so all my students had access to a school-provided laptop, which allowed me to begin my career with a strong technology emphasis. Edmodo was the primary Learning Management System used.

In addition to teaching I was also the FCCLA sponsor. During my time in Holcomb we were able to increase FCCLA membership from 6 to 25 students. In my second year, I also created a “Sunshine Committee” for the staff to boost morale and increase building engagement.
